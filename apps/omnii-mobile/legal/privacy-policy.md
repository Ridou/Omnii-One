# OMNII Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 30, 2025  
**Last Updated:** May 30, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to OMNII ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application OMNII (the "App") and related services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App. This policy outlines our data practices in compliance with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, full name (optional)
- **Profile Information**: Work preferences, productivity patterns, AI persona settings
- **Communication Data**: Messages in chat interface, feedback submissions
- **Productivity Data**: Task completion patterns, usage analytics, achievement progress

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Information**: App usage patterns, feature interactions, session duration
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, network information
- **Location Data**: General location (city/country level) for analytics purposes only

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Google OAuth**: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture
- **Integration Data**: Data from connected productivity tools (with your explicit consent)

## 3. Google Services Integration

OMNII integrates with Google services to provide enhanced productivity features. When you grant permission, we access the following Google services with these specific scopes:

### 3.1 Basic Profile Information
**Scopes**: `openid`, `profile`, `email`
- **Access**: Your basic Google account information
- **Usage**: Account authentication, profile display, and user identification
- **Data Collected**: Email address, name, profile picture
- **Storage**: Profile information stored in your OMNII account

### 3.2 Gmail Integration
**Scopes**: `https://mail.google.com/`, `gmail.readonly`, `gmail.compose`, `gmail.send`, `gmail.modify`, `gmail.labels`
- **Access**: Full read and write access to your Gmail account
- **Usage**: 
  - Analyze email content for AI-powered task suggestions
  - Compose and send emails on your behalf when approved
  - Organize emails with labels and filters
  - Provide smart email management and automated responses
  - Extract actionable items from your inbox
- **Data Processing**: Email content analyzed by AI for productivity insights
- **Storage**: Email metadata cached temporarily, full content not permanently stored
- **User Control**: All email actions require your explicit approval before execution

### 3.3 Google Drive Integration
**Scopes**: `drive`, `drive.file`, `drive.metadata`
- **Access**: Read, create, and modify files in your Google Drive
- **Usage**:
  - Organize documents and files for better productivity
  - Analyze document content for task suggestions
  - Create and update productivity-related documents
  - Provide intelligent file management recommendations
- **Data Processing**: Document metadata and content analysis for productivity insights
- **Storage**: File metadata cached for organization features
- **User Control**: File modifications require explicit user approval

### 3.4 Google Calendar Integration  
**Scopes**: `calendar`, `calendar.events`, `calendar.readonly`
- **Access**: Read and modify your calendar events and schedules
- **Usage**: 
  - Analyze schedule patterns for optimal time management
  - Suggest meeting times and schedule optimization
  - Create calendar events for tasks and deadlines
  - Provide intelligent scheduling recommendations
  - Integrate tasks with your existing calendar
- **Data Processing**: Calendar data analyzed for scheduling optimization
- **Storage**: Calendar metadata cached temporarily for real-time suggestions
- **User Control**: Calendar modifications require user approval

### 3.5 Google Contacts Integration
**Scopes**: `contacts`, `contacts.readonly`, `contacts.other.readonly`
- **Access**: Read your Google contacts and contact information
- **Usage**:
  - Suggest contact information for task assignments
  - Facilitate collaboration and communication features
  - Provide context for email and calendar integrations
  - Enable smart contact suggestions in productivity workflows
- **Data Processing**: Contact information analyzed for collaboration suggestions
- **Storage**: Contact metadata cached for feature functionality
- **Privacy**: Contact information never shared with third parties

### 3.6 Google Sheets Integration
**Scopes**: `spreadsheets`, `spreadsheets.readonly`
- **Access**: Read and modify Google Sheets spreadsheets
- **Usage**:
  - Extract data for productivity tracking and analytics
  - Create and update productivity dashboards
  - Analyze spreadsheet data for insights and task suggestions
  - Integrate spreadsheet data with task management features
- **Data Processing**: Spreadsheet content analyzed for productivity optimization
- **Storage**: Spreadsheet metadata cached for integration features
- **User Control**: Spreadsheet modifications require explicit approval

### 3.7 Google Tasks Integration
**Scopes**: `tasks`, `tasks.readonly`
- **Access**: Read and modify your Google Tasks
- **Usage**: 
  - Sync tasks between OMNII and Google Tasks platforms
  - Provide unified task management across all platforms
  - Analyze task completion patterns for productivity insights
- **Data Processing**: Task data synchronized bidirectionally
- **Storage**: Task data synchronized and stored in both platforms
- **User Control**: Task modifications can be controlled through app settings

### 3.8 Data Retention for Google Services
- **Retention Period**: Google user data is retained only as long as necessary to provide requested features
- **User Control**: You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com
- **Data Deletion**: Upon revocation, we delete all associated Google data within 30 days
- **Minimal Data**: We store only the minimum data necessary for app functionality
- **No Sale**: We never sell, rent, or trade your Google data to third parties

### 3.9 Security and Encryption
- **Data Transmission**: All Google API communications use HTTPS encryption
- **Data Storage**: Google data stored with industry-standard encryption
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limit who can view your Google data
- **Audit Logs**: All Google data access is logged and monitored

### 3.10 User Rights and Control
- **Granular Control**: You can manage permissions for each Google service individually
- **Approval System**: Most Google data actions require your explicit approval
- **Data Export**: You can export your data at any time
- **Account Deletion**: You can delete your account and all Google data associations
- **Transparency**: You can view all Google data access in your account dashboard

# Omnii SMS Consent Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 15, 2025

## Overview
This document outlines Omnii's SMS consent collection and management practices in compliance with applicable regulations including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and Cellular Telecommunications Industry Association (CTIA) guidelines.

## Consent Collection Method

### When Consent is Collected
SMS consent is collected during the mobile app registration process when users create their Omnii account.

### How Consent is Collected
Users must actively check a consent checkbox during registration. The consent is not pre-checked and requires explicit user action.

### Consent Language
The exact consent language presented to users is:

> "I agree to receive SMS notifications from Omnii about task updates, approvals, and important account information. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time."

## Types of SMS Messages

### Transactional Messages
- Task approval notifications
- Account security alerts
- System maintenance notifications
- Password reset confirmations

### Informational Messages
- Feature updates
- Service announcements
- Account status changes

## Opt-Out Procedures

### Immediate Opt-Out
Users can opt out immediately by replying "STOP" to any SMS message from Omnii.

### App Settings
Users can disable SMS notifications through the app settings menu:
1. Open Omnii app
2. Navigate to Settings
3. Select Notifications
4. Toggle off SMS notifications

## Data Retention
- Consent records are retained for 3 years after account closure
- Opt-out requests are processed immediately and records retained permanently
- Message logs are retained for 1 year for troubleshooting purposes
